Proceeding in a sequential hierarchy.
We've discovered that the ideal content solutions necessitate three key components: strategy, technology, and talent. We combine all three on a single platform, allowing you to create content that both delights and drives results. Our procedure goes in the following order:
Gather Information

Our first step in content development is always to collect as much information and dig out minor or major loopholes in previous content as possible.

Blog Topic Analysis

We examine which pages on a competitor's site generate the most organic traffic. We'll assess whether we can outrank the competition based on the backlink profile, length, and quality of those pages.

Strategize and Curate

During the content interpretation phase, we develop the content strategy and editorial calendar. Polus Technologies’ team produces pieces of high-quality content that satisfy search intent and that will encourage the reader to share.

Optimize & Publish

Once the curation phase is complete, we move on to optimization and publishing.t’s here that we consider things like readability, word count, related keywords, and more to make the content more attractive to search engines and audiences.

Promote

Social media is one thing, we move towards republishing platforms, email outreach, backlinks, and mining gold in the SEO industry.

Review & Repeat

Content solutions are never "done" or "merely provided." It is a continuous process that begins as soon as it concludes. As a result, we evaluate our content marketing efforts for each website on a monthly basis.
